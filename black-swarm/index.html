Westside Black Swarm

“Fortis Fortuna Adiuvat”

“Fortune Favors the Bold”

We are Westside Black Swarm, a High School (grades 9-12) boys tackle rugby club located on the west side of Indianapolis.

We welcome new or experienced players to join.

The Westside Black Swarm (previously known as the Avon Rugby Black Swarm) was founded in 2009 and joined the Westside Rugby Football Club family in 2021.

We have regularly been nationally ranked since 2021, with multiple players being selected to the All Star Roster for Indiana Rugby.

Our seasons for High School are spring 15s and fall 7s.

Our High School Boys were Indiana State Champions for 2022 and 2023 Fall 7s.
