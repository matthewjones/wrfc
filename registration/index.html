Registration Help

Westside Rugby Football Club is part of Rugby Indiana.

Rugby Indiana uses the Rugby Xplorer Registration Tool.

 

Please see the information below for registration instructions.

Register Now
  1. From https://westsiderugbyfootballclub.org/
    1. Click on the “Register Now” Button
    2. Click on the “Login” tile.
    3. Sign in with the username and password that you set up when registering you and/or your child for the Rugby club. (This sign-in is the “Rugby Xplorer” account.)
  2. Once signed into your Rugby Xplorer account, click “Register” from the left-hand side menu.
    1. From the “Play Rugby” menu, click on the “Find a Club” tile.
    2. Choose the player you want to register from the listed people and click “Next”.
    3. Search for “Westside Rugby”. When it appears, choose it and click “Next”.
    4. Choose the appropriate Role, Registration Type, and Duration of “Season”. Click “Next”.
    5. Continue filling out the form and submit.
