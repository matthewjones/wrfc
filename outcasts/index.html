Westside Outcasts

“Fortis Fortuna Adiuvat”

“Fortune Favors the Bold”

We are the Westside Outcasts, a Division IV men’s rugby club located on the west side of Indianapolis.

We welcome new or experienced players to join.

We became part of Westside Rugby Football Club family in 2021.

Our seasons are in the spring and fall.
